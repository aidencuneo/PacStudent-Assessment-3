Design Innovation:

I created a gamemode where only a small area on the screen is visible. The idea
is that the player is mining in a cave with limited visibility, and when they
come across power pellets, it charges up their flashlight, allowing them to see
further. The ghosts alternate between moving in a random direction and moving
towards the player, and they have the same speed as the player at all times.
They are also able to teleport just out of view of the player to attempt to
jumpscare them. Ghosts can not be killed, but they still appear scared because
they don't like the light. This gamemode is supposed to be quite hard compared
to the original game. Lots of other little things have been changed, such as
diamonds spawning around the map instead of flying through it.
